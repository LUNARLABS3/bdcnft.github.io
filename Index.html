ROADMAP

Our vision for the BDC-NFT project is to create a dynamic and engaging NFT platform that provides a unique and rewarding experience for collectors and creators alike. Here's what we have planned:

Phase 1: Platform Development

  • ➤Create an intuitive and user-friendly NFT creation tool that allows artists to easily create and upload their work to the platform.
  • ➤Develop an advanced search and discovery system that makes it easy for collectors to find the NFTs they are interested in.
  • ➤Implement smart contract technology that allows creators to earn royalties on secondary sales of their NFTs.

Phase 2: Community Growth

  • ➤Host regular events and contests that encourage creators to showcase their work and engage with the community.
  • ➤Offer educational resources and tutorials for artists and collectors who are new to NFTs, helping to grow the overall knowledge and interest in the space.
  • ➤Create a referral program that rewards users for bringing new creators and collectors to the platform.

Phase 3: Platform Expansion

  • ➤Explore partnerships with other NFT marketplaces to expand the reach of BDC-NFT and create more opportunities for creators to showcase their work.
  • ➤Develop mobile apps that allow users to buy, sell, and trade NFTs on the go.
  • ➤Expand into new industries and niches, such as sports memorabilia, music, and gaming, to attract a wider audience and diversify the types of NFTs available on the platform.
